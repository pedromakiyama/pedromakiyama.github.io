About

I am Pedro Paulino Makiyama

I’m a passionate 14-year-old comic book writer and illustrator. I’m fascinated by the intersection of art and science and envision a future where art can help us better connect with nature. My goal is to collaborate on stories that challenge our fundamental relationship with nature, humanity, and life itself. I dream of a world of kindness and collaboration. The world is so much better when we are good with each other!
